About SATORI

SATORI is a Japanese floating lantern festival centered around self-discovery, reflection, and renewal. Established since 2015, Satori has expanded across the United States to offer participants a space to pause, breathe, and reconnect with themselves through a symbolic act of release.

What to Expect

Reflective Atmosphere

Unlike traditional festivals filled with noise and lights, Satori invites participants to slow down, reflect, and connect through quiet release and shared presence under a sky of floating lanterns.

Guided Intentions

Before the lanterns take flight, guests are guided through a brief mindfulness exercise — writing thoughts, goals, or memories that they wish to release or honor.

Community and Connection

Each event concludes in shared silence, as hundreds of lanterns drift upward — a moment of unity and introspection, reminding everyone of our shared hopes and humanity.
