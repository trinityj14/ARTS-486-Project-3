SATORI Kyoto

Rooted in centuries of Japanese tradition, SATORI Kyoto honors the spirit of togetherness and mindfulness. Set within the historic Kamo River Park, the festival invites guests to reflect, connect, and send forth their wishes through the soft glow of lanterns drifting skyward.

Date: Friday, August 15, 2025

Time: 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM
